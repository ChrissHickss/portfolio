Who is Chris Hicks?

I am currently serving in the United States Air Force as an Enterprise Logistics Manager for the KC-135. In other words, I oversee the entire Air Force's KC-135 fleet and ensure that they are getting the right plane parts at the right time in order to sustain the Air Force mission. Enough of the boring stuff though.

I completed a Full-Stack Software Engineering bootcamp called Code Platoon this past year and will also be finishing up my Bachelor's in Data Science from Arizona State University in Spring 2023. I have plans to pursue either a Master's in Business Analytics or in Computer Science in the near future.

I am intrigued by data and how it can tell many different stories depneding on who is looking at it. I am breaking into the datasphere from a non-technical background, which from my point of view gives me a unique advantage in that I have extensive domain knowledge in Supply Chain and Logistics that I can leverage to gain valuable insights.

While data excites me in a professional sense, I also have a life in the wild. Three things I couldn't live without besides my family are Ice Hockey, lifting weights, and fishing. I also have the coolest dog I've ever met. He's a dalmatian. His brother is cool too I guess, he's not a dalmatian.
